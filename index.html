Le Duc Huy

I am pleased to listen, collaborate, and share my experience on how to actively research on health data to make a right health decision.

Dr. Huy is a medical doctor in preventive medicine, a lecturer, and a researcher at Health Management Training Institute, Hue University of Medicine and Pharmacy, Vietnam. He completed the fellowship program given by the International Society for Quality in Health Care in 2021. Currently, he is pursuing the M.B.A Degree in the Master Program of Health Care Administration, Taipei Medical University, Taiwan. He is also a key coordinator in the first aid project, which conducted evaluation studies and provided free training courses for over 800 Vietnamese students between 2020 and 2021. In addition, he actively participated in and reported at various international conferences regarding public health, health literacy, patient safety, and health policy. His research focuses on health literacy, bibliometric study, health policy, adolescent health, and application of artificial intelligence in health care management.
